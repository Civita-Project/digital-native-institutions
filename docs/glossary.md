# Glossary

This glossary defines core concepts used in Digital Native Institutions (DNI) and the National Service Unit (NSU).

---

### **Status Function**
Mapping:  
`X counts as Y in context C`  
The foundational mechanism by which institutional facts are created.  
See `/theory/ontology.md`.

---

### **Institutional Fact**
A fact that exists because a community accepts a status function as binding.

---

### **MVLP (Minimum Viable Legal Persona)**
The minimal identity attributes needed to participate in institutional workflows.

---

### **Attestation**
Evidence submitted by a human, device, or institution, with liability weight and provenance.

---

### **Document Lineage**
Hash-linked version history of documents ensuring integrity and auditability.

---

### **State Transition**
Deterministic change from one institutional state to another, triggered by valid evidence and rules.

---

Additional terms will be added as the dissertation expands.
