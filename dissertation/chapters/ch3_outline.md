**Intent**
Position Chapter 3 as the methodological bridge between the execution-failure diagnosis (Chapter 1) and the literature-mapped gap (Chapter 2), without introducing solutions.

**In-scope**

* Restatement of the dissertation’s epistemic problem as *methodological insufficiency*, not design absence
* Clarification of why existing methods cannot adjudicate execution-level institutional claims
* Orientation to what Chapter 3 will formally establish

**Explicit Boundaries**

* No theory extension
* No system design
* No empirical material
* No evaluation results

**Out-of-scope**

* Architecture
* Prototypes
* Case studies
* Policy relevance

**Role in Dissertation Flow**

* Re-anchors the reader before formal definitions and methods are introduced
* Prevents misreading Chapter 3 as a “solution chapter”
